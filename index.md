# Privacy Policy for Atlas of the Depths

**Last Updated: December 31, 2025**

**Developer: Keckmate Games**

## Introduction

Atlas of the Depths ("the Game") is developed by Keckmate Games ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

**Advertising Data**: The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Account credentials
- Payment information (in-app purchases are handled by Google Play)
- Location data beyond what AdMob collects
- Contacts or personal files
- Gameplay data or save files (stored locally on your device only)

## How We Use Information

We do not directly collect or use any personal information. Third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) use collected data to:
- Display relevant advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud

## Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and save data are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data and it is not transmitted to any servers.

## Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

**Google Play Services**

## Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game displays non-personalized ads when possible.

## Ad-Free Option

Users may purchase an in-app upgrade to remove all advertisements. This eliminates AdMob data collection within the Game.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
- Reset your advertising identifier
- Delete local game data by uninstalling the Game
